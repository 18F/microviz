Open Opportunities Platform

The Open Opportunities is a node.js-based platform to foster collaboration on government projects. This issue seeks to create a new user role for “Agency Admins” that will (in the future) give those users admin privileges to see data for users from their agency.

Tock

Tock is a simple time tracking app that 18F employees use to report their time on a weekly basis. Currently, no user may enter more than 40 hours per week. This issue seeks to allow certain specified users of Tock to enter anywhere between 0 and 60 hours.
